Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

The short version: klauskode.com is a static website. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and uses no advertising or tracking. The only data that leaves your browser does so to deliver the page itself — your IP address is necessarily seen by the hosting provider (GitHub). The site's fonts are self-hosted, so nothing is loaded from Google.

This policy explains what happens to your data when you visit klauskode.com (the “Site”). It is written with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in mind, since the Site is operated from Germany.

# What this Site does not do

  • No cookies are set.
  • No analytics or statistics tools (no Google Analytics, Plausible, Matomo, etc.).
  • No advertising, retargeting, or tracking pixels.
  • No accounts, logins, or contact forms.
  • No data is sold or shared for marketing purposes.

# Hosting and server logs (GitHub Pages)

The Site is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service of GitHub, Inc. (a Microsoft company). When you load a page, your browser sends a request to GitHub's servers, which — like virtually all web servers — may automatically process technical data such as your IP address, the requested URL, the date and time, the referring page, and your browser/operating system (user agent). This processing is carried out by GitHub and is necessary to transmit the website to you securely.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating and securing the website). For details on how GitHub handles this data, see the GitHub Privacy Statement and the GitHub Pages data collection notice.

# Fonts

The “Inter” and “JetBrains Mono” typefaces used on this Site are self-hosted — the font files are served directly from this Site's own hosting and are not loaded from Google Fonts or any other third party. No data is transmitted to an external font provider when you view a page.

# Contacting us by email

If you email us using a link on this Site, your email address and the contents of your message are processed solely to handle your enquiry (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f) GDPR). We keep such correspondence only as long as needed to respond and to meet any legal retention obligations.

# External links

This Site links to third-party websites (for example GitHub, LynxLabs.ai, and other projects). Once you follow such a link, this policy no longer applies — please review the privacy policy of each site you visit.

# Data retention

This Site itself stores no personal data. Any technical log data is retained by the hosting and font providers according to their own policies, linked above.

# Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your personal data, and to object to processing based on legitimate interests. Where processing relies on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email above.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. For Berlin, this is the Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit.

# Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the Site or to legal requirements. The “last updated” date at the top indicates the current version.
